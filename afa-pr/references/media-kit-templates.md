# 媒体套件与新闻稿模板 (Media Kit & Press Release Templates)

> **核心理念**：当记者对你的 Pitch 感兴趣时，他们需要尽快获取高分辨率图片、品牌背景和准确的数据。一个专业的媒体套件（Media Kit）能将他们写稿的摩擦力降到最低。

## 一、标准化媒体套件 (Media Kit) 结构

一个完整的数字媒体套件通常是一个托管在云盘（如 Google Drive, Dropbox）或官网隐藏页面（如 `brand.com/press`）的文件夹。

### 1.1 核心文件夹结构

```text
/BrandName_Media_Kit
├── 1_Company_Overview.pdf      # 公司简介、使命与核心数据
├── 2_Founder_Bios.pdf          # 创始人简介与高管照片
├── 3_Logos_and_Brand_Assets/   # 各种格式的 Logo (PNG, SVG, 黑白/彩色)
├── 4_Product_Images/           # 产品图 (白底图 + Lifestyle 场景图)
├── 5_Press_Releases/           # 过往重要新闻稿存档
└── 6_Media_Coverage/           # 过往重要媒体报道截图/链接
```

### 1.2 公司简介 (Company Boilerplate) 模板

*Boilerplate 是一段标准化的公司介绍，通常放在所有新闻稿的末尾，或作为媒体套件的核心文档。*

**[Brand Name] Company Overview**

**The Elevator Pitch (一句话介绍):**
[Brand Name] is a [Category] brand that helps [Target Audience] achieve [Core Benefit] through [Unique Mechanism].

**Our Mission (我们的使命):**
Founded in [Year] by [Founder Name(s)], [Brand Name] was born out of a simple belief: [Core Belief/Problem to Solve]. We are on a mission to [Long-term Vision].

**Fast Facts (核心数据):**
- **Founded**: [Year]
- **Headquarters**: [City, State/Country]
- **Flagship Product**: [Hero Product Name]
- **Key Milestones**: [e.g., Over 1 million units sold / Featured in Vogue, Forbes / Raised $X in funding]
- **Sustainability/Impact**: [e.g., plastic-reduced packaging / a defined share of profits donated to X / certified impact initiative]

**Media Contact (媒体联系人):**
Name: [PR Contact Name]
Email: [PR Email, e.g., press@brand.com]
Website: [Website URL]
Social: [@BrandHandle]

### 1.3 创始人简介 (Founder Bio) 模板

**[Founder Name], [Title] of [Brand Name]**

**Short Bio (短版 - 50字以内):**
[Founder Name] is the [Title] and founder of [Brand Name], a [Category] company dedicated to [Mission]. Prior to launching [Brand Name], [He/She/They] spent [X] years in [Previous Industry/Role], where [He/She/They] discovered the need for [Product Solution].

**Long Bio (长版 - 150-200字):**
[Founder Name] is the visionary behind [Brand Name], the innovative [Category] brand that is disrupting how we [Core Action]. 

The idea for [Brand Name] came to [Founder Name] when [Describe the "Aha!" moment or personal struggle]. Frustrated by the lack of [Specific feature/solution] on the market, [He/She/They] spent [Time period] developing a prototype that would eventually become [Hero Product].

Before founding [Brand Name] in [Year], [Founder Name] built a successful career in [Previous Industry], working with [Notable past companies or achievements]. This background gave [Him/Her/Them] unique insights into [Relevant skill, e.g., supply chain, consumer psychology].

Today, [Founder Name] leads a team of [Number] and is passionate about [Personal passion related to the brand, e.g., sustainable manufacturing, empowering women in tech]. When not working on [Brand Name], [He/She/They] can be found [Personal hobby/interest].

## 二、新闻稿 (Press Release) 撰写指南与模板

虽然在 2026 年，精准的冷推（Cold Pitching）比群发新闻稿更有效，但在**重大里程碑**（如新品发布、重大融资、重要联名合作）时，一份标准的新闻稿仍然是必要的官方记录，也有助于 SEO（通过 PR Newswire 等渠道分发）。

### 2.1 新闻稿的 5W1H 原则
- **Who**: 谁发布的新闻？涉及哪些关键人物？
- **What**: 发生了什么事？（新品、融资、合作）
- **When**: 什么时候发生的？（或什么时候上市？）
- **Where**: 在哪里可以买到/看到？
- **Why**: 为什么这件事很重要？（解决了什么行业痛点？）
- **How**: 它是如何运作的？

### 2.2 标准新闻稿模板（新品发布）

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**[Brand Name] Launches [Product Name]: The First [Unique Descriptor] Designed for [Target Audience]**

*[City, State] – [Date]* – Today, [Brand Name], the [Category] company known for [Brand's main claim to fame], announced the launch of [Product Name]. This new offering is designed to solve [Specific Problem], providing [Target Audience] with a [Key Benefit] way to [Core Action].

Unlike traditional [Competitor Category], which often [Mention a common flaw, e.g., use toxic chemicals / break easily], [Product Name] features [Unique Mechanism/Feature 1] and [Unique Mechanism/Feature 2]. 

"We created [Product Name] because we were tired of seeing our customers struggle with [Pain Point]," said [Founder Name], [Title] of [Brand Name]. "Our goal was to engineer a solution that not only works better but also aligns with our commitment to [Brand Value, e.g., sustainability/affordability]. We spent [Time period] perfecting the design, and we couldn't be more thrilled to finally share it with the world."

**Key features of [Product Name] include:**
- **[Feature 1]**: [Brief explanation of benefit]
- **[Feature 2]**: [Brief explanation of benefit]
- **[Feature 3]**: [Brief explanation of benefit]

[Product Name] is available starting today, [Date], exclusively at [Website URL] [and/or mention retail partners]. It retails for $[Price] and comes in [Mention colors/sizes/variants].

For more information about [Product Name] or to request a sample for review, please contact [PR Contact Name] at [Email Address]. High-resolution images and the full media kit can be accessed here: [Link to Media Kit].

**About [Brand Name]:**
[Insert the Company Boilerplate here]

**Media Contact:**
[Name]
[Title]
[Email]
[Phone Number]
[Website]

### 2.3 新闻稿分发建议
- **不要**：只把新闻稿发到 PR Newswire 上然后祈祷有人看。
- **要**：将新闻稿作为附件或链接，配合 CPR 框架的 Pitch 邮件，单独发送给你精心挑选的一小批高相关目标记者。
- **SEO 价值**：在官网建立一个 "Press" 或 "Newsroom" 页面，将所有新闻稿发布在上面，这有助于提升品牌相关关键词的搜索排名。
